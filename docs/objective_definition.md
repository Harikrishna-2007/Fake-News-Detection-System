# Objective Definition – Fake News Detection System

## Project Title

Fake News Detection System

## Problem Statement

The rapid spread of fake news through social media and online platforms can mislead people and create misinformation. It is difficult for users to manually verify the authenticity of every news article.

## Main Objective

To develop a Machine Learning-based Fake News Detection System that automatically classifies news articles as Real or Fake.

## Specific Objectives

1. Collect and analyze fake and real news datasets.
2. Preprocess news text using Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques.
3. Extract meaningful features from news content.
4. Train Machine Learning models for fake news classification.
5. Predict whether a news article is Real or Fake.
6. Provide a user-friendly interface for news verification.
7. Improve awareness and reduce the spread of misinformation.

## Expected Outcome

The system should accurately analyze news content and provide a prediction indicating whether the news is real or fake.
